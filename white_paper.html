This ‘block’ chronologically stores information on all the transactions that take place in the chain. Hence, the name blockchain is a database with stamped and immutable time information of each transaction replicated on servers around the world. This technology is the basis of bitcoin, a cryptographic currency, the most famous and strongest virtual currency or cryptocurrency.

The Blockchain system is therefore based on a network of nodes, that is, a decentralized network of nodes (virtual machines with direct connection input/output or physical hosts) equal connected between which the information is distributed, and, since there is no central entity, it is the same network that verifies the transactions that are executed.
